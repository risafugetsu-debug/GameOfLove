## 1. Overview

Game of Love ("the App") is a personal relationship tracking game. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2a. Account Data (via Clerk)
When you create an account or sign in, authentication is handled by Clerk (clerk.com). Clerk may collect:
- Email address
- Name (if provided during sign-up)
- OAuth profile information (if you sign in with Google, Apple, etc.)

Clerk's privacy policy applies to this data: https://clerk.com/privacy

### 2b. Data You Enter in the App
The following data is stored **locally on your device** using SQLite and is not transmitted to our servers:
- Names and photos of people you add to your board
- Date log entries (notes, vibes, timestamps)
- Board positions and game progress

### 2c. Photos
If you add a photo to a person's profile, the App requests access to your photo library. Photos are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload photos to any server.

### 2d. Data We Do NOT Collect
- Location data
- Contacts
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- **Account data** is used solely for authentication (logging you in and out)
- **App data** (names, logs, photos) stays on your device and is used only to run the App
- We do not sell, share, or monetize any of your personal data

## 5. Data Storage & Security

All personal data you enter (people, logs, game progress) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It is not backed up to our servers. If you delete the App, this data is permanently deleted.

Account credentials are managed by Clerk and are subject to their security practices.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at risa.editorial.project@gmail.com.

## 7. Your Rights

You may:
- **Delete your account** at any time from within the App, which removes your authentication data from Clerk
- **Delete all local data** by uninstalling the App
- **Contact us** at risa.editorial.project@gmail.com with any privacy questions or requests
