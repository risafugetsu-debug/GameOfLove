1. Overview

Game of Love is a personal relationship tracking game. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

Data You Enter in the App
The following is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Names and photos of people you add to your board
  • Date log entries (notes, vibes, timestamps)
  • Board positions and game progress

Photos
If you add a photo to a person's profile, the app requests access to your photo library. Photos are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload photos to any server.

Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect any personal data, account information, location data, contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers. The app requires no account and no internet connection to function.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data stays on your device and is used only to run the app. We do not sell, share, or monetize any of your data — because we never receive it.

4. Third-Party Services

Game of Love does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data you enter is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It is never transmitted or backed up to any server. If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you have concerns, contact us at risa.editorial.project@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights

  • Delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us with any privacy questions or requests

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above.
