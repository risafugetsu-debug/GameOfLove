1. Overview

Game of Love is a personal relationship tracking game. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

Account Data (via Clerk)
When you create an account or sign in, authentication is handled by Clerk (clerk.com). Clerk may collect your email address, name, and OAuth profile information. Clerk's privacy policy applies to this data.

Data You Enter in the App
The following is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Names and photos of people you add to your board
  • Date log entries (notes, vibes, timestamps)
  • Board positions and game progress

Photos
If you add a photo to a person's profile, the app requests access to your photo library. Photos are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload photos to any server.

Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Data

Account data is used solely for authentication. App data (names, logs, photos) stays on your device and is used only to run the app. We do not sell, share, or monetize any of your personal data.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal data you enter is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It is not backed up to our servers. If you delete the app, this data is permanently deleted. Account credentials are managed by Clerk.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at risa.editorial.project@gmail.com.
